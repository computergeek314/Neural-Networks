Visterm

Visterm is a terminal application which adds an audio device to the right-hand side of the terminal. It uses SDL to open the default audio device, and it does a fast fourier transform to convert the audio data into the frequency/amplitude graph that is displayed as the moving bars on the right. In addition, the application opens a pseudo-terminal device under the POSIX standard and relays the user's input to the new terminal and the output back to the display.
