Terminal Rubik's Cube

This rubiks cube shader scrambles the cube to the superflip position and solves it repeatedly. The superflip takes 20 moves to solve, which is the most moves any position on a rubiks cube takes. It uses Phong shading for the lighting and traces rays from the surface of the cube to the point light source to produce shadows.
